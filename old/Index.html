Traditional Chinese Medicine
Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) is a holistic healthcare system which is discovered and practiced in China for thousands of years, it is not only popular in China, but also in other countries in Asia, such as Japan, Korea, Malaysia, Singapore etc. In recent years, it has gained popularity also in the western worlds. Traditional Chinese Medicine promotes, maintains and restores our health through adjusting the balance of our body's energy by acupuncture and Herbs.

Acupuncture
Acupuncture is one major TCM therapy that restores our health by stimulating the body's certain places (called acupuncture points) with fine, disposable stainless steel needles. TCM believes that the normal health depends on the normal flow of the body's energy. Discomfort and problems are caused when the body's energy flow is disturbed. Using acupuncture by inserting needles to certain points, natural balance of the body's energy can be restored.

Chinese Herbs
Chinese herbs are also called Chinese herb medicines. It is another major therapy that uses plant elements (roots, leaves, flowers and seeds), animal and mineral products to rebalance the body's energy and restore the good health. Chinese herbs maybe administered as raw ingredients and boiled in hot water, powders to mix with water, ready-made capsules, pills or tablets. Nowadays many new products of Chinese herbs have good results in reducing high blood cholesterol, good for kidney or urinary tract stones, prostatitis etc.
