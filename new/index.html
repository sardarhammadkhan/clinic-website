About Us

Chinese Acupuncture Centre and Chinese Clinical Lab Islamabad is established in 1991, it is named by health and family planning commission of Gansu Province of China and Gansu Provincial Hospital of TCM as “Qi-Huang Chinese Medicine Centre of Pakistan”. As a reputed international medical centre of TCM, it is well intergraded by western medicine with Chinese natural herb medicine, we have well equipped laboratory that can perform various types of tests, including different blood tests and urine tests etc. We also have X-ray, ECG and ultra sound facilities. With all these facilities and equipment we provide diagnosis, treatment and general medical examination. Our staff is qualified and experienced.
